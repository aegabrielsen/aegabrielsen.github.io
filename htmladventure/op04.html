You get one last good night of sleep and then head out of town as soon as morning breaks. You travel the dusty roads with nothing but your supplies and covered wagon. After a while of traveling you come to a river. The path leads through it, but the bridge is broken! There is another road going along the river, if you follow it long enough you will likely find another crossing, but it could take a long time. What do you do?
